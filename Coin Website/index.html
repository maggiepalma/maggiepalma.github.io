COIN is an American pop rock band formed in 2012 in Nashville, Tennessee. Ryan Winnen (left), Chase Lawrence (middle), and Joe Memmel (right) join together to create a synth pop sound that is practically unheard of in Nashville.

UNCANNY VALLEY

The fourth album from the band is the most experiential so far.The album is a narrative story where the character of the album is stuck within artificial intelligence. However, the album ended up being about human experience with breakdowns of the memories and emotions of events that influenced the band.
